## Frequently Asked Questions

### Appointments & Booking
**Q: How far in advance should I book?**
A: We recommend booking 1-2 weeks in advance, especially for weekends and special nail art services.

**Q: What is your cancellation policy?**
A: Please give us at least 24 hours notice for cancellations to avoid a cancellation fee.

### Services & Pricing
**Q: How long do gel manicures last?**
A: Our gel manicures typically last 2-3 weeks with proper care and maintenance.

**Q: Do you use non-toxic nail products?**
A: Yes! We prioritize your health and use high-quality, non-toxic nail polishes and treatments whenever possible.

**Q: Can you create custom nail art designs?**
A: Absolutely! Our nail artists love creating custom designs. Bring your inspiration photos or describe your vision.

### Nail Care & Health
**Q: How often should I get a manicure?**
A: We recommend professional manicures every 2-3 weeks to maintain healthy nails and cuticles.

**Q: Do you offer nail repair services?**
A: Yes, we provide nail repair and strengthening treatments for damaged or broken nails.

**Q: What should I do to maintain my nails between visits?**
A: Use cuticle oil daily, wear gloves when cleaning, and avoid using your nails as tools.

### Special Services
**Q: Do you provide bridal nail services?**
A: Yes! We offer bridal packages and can coordinate nail designs for wedding parties.

**Q: Can you match specific colors?**
A: We have an extensive color selection and can often match specific shades. Bring a reference if you have one!
